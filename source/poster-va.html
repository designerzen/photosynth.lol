Smile Powered Music : The Sympathetic Synthesizer

Collaborative music making with moods!

Explore adaptive instruments and inclusive digital tools that make music-making accessible to everyone. Learn how designers create tools for different needs, then experiment with sounds, and rhythms to build your own track. A fun, hands-on way to understand the power of adaptive design through creativity and play.

This is a free, drop-in activity for families to enjoy together. Please note: places are limited and allocated on a first come first served basis. Suitable for ages 5+, and no child can be left unattended. Last entry is 30 minutes before closing time, no booking is required.

Activity developed with Dave Darch and Francesca Gkotsi.
