The PhotoSYNTH is the result of a long term collaboration between Drake Music's legendary research and development team DMLab, and zen Olenski from Awesomething Social Enterprise. Together they have made magical musical bowler hats, beard organs, a whole lotta gloves, and various other experimental interfaces, some of which have ended up in the hands of disabled artists and musicians in studios, bedrooms and classrooms around the world.
