July 1st, 2025

The Summer Science Exhibition is a free six-day festival showcasing the science shaping our future and will be held at the Royal Society in London from July 1st to July 6th, 2025.

Come and join us for a fun musical celebration of inclusive technology, play with some of the newest instruments from the Accessible Music Instrument Collection and learn all about the research and science that made them possible.

The exhibition will feature a series of interactive installations, hands-on demonstrations, musical performances and talks.

Located in the heart of London

Between Buckingham Palace and Trafalgar Square, the Royal Society is located in a fully accessible building that is wheelchair friendly and has hearing loops enabled, quiet spaces and a cafe.
