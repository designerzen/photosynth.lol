Thursday 30th October 2025, 10:00am - 16:00pm

Take part in an inclusive, multi-sensory music experience at SHINE West Bowling.

Come and join us for a fun musical celebration of inclusive technology, play with some of the newest instruments from the Accessible Music Instrument Collection and learn all about the research and science that made them possible.

The exhibition will feature a series of interactive installations, hands-on demonstrations, musical performances and talks.

Located in a beautiful church in Bradford

The event explores our connection with music, with a focus on innovative tech and accessibility in live music.
