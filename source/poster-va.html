Smile Powered Music : The Sympathetic Synthesizer

Instant Musical Superpowers!

Rather than playing a musical instrument, this musical instrument plays you! Use your facial expressions and movements to create pop music that matches your moods!

This accessible and inclusive music making activity will put a smile on your face and make it musical =P

This is a free, drop-in activity, suitable for all ages to enjoy together, where up to four people collaborate to simultaneously make new music. Have fun becoming a pop star. No booking required.

Activity developed by Zenon Olenski with support from Sound Connections, Drake Music and MASSIG.
